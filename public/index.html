The Best Movies According To Sreeram

My Top 3 Movies Of All Time:

Me before you

Me Before You is a heartfelt film about Louisa Clark, a cheerful caregiver, and Will Traynor, a man paralyzed after an accident. Their relationship, filled with both warmth and sorrow, explores love, independence, and life’s tough choices in a deeply moving way.

Your Name

Your Name is a visually stunning film where two teenagers, Taki and Mitsuha, mysteriously swap bodies and form a deep connection. Blending romance and fantasy, it delivers an emotionally resonant story that stays with you long after it ends.

I Saw the Devil

I Saw the Devil is a brutal and gripping thriller about a secret agent’s relentless quest for revenge against a serial killer. The film explores the dark side of human nature, leaving a lasting, chilling impact.
